Prerna's old Age Home' & Welfare Society (POAHWS), is a 3 decade old non-profit organisation, that cares and works for abandoned, destitute, and homeless elderly in Delhi NCR and Uttar Pradesh. Through three old-age homes, POAHWS provides free food, medical care, and shelter to over 500 seniors.
